Songbooks

Piece by piece we have begun the work of transcribing songs from the Dunkard songbooks, and also collecting suitable Dutch songs and translations. By writing the notes in MuseScore, it is possible to listen to each of the four parts separately or together, so that you can learn to sing in harmony.
