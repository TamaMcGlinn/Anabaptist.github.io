Christian confession

We do believe in the one almighty loving God who created the heavens and the earth. We believe in His Son Jesus Christ who gave His life and thereby bought forgiveness for every sinner. We believe Jesus was raised from the dead after three days and continued to preach and exhort the saints to do good, and to spread the gospel all over the world. After He rose up to heaven, the Holy Ghost still remains to remind us of the presence of God and to instill in us the love to do great deeds in His name. Our love of the Lord and of all the people of the world is the fullfillment of the most important commandment.

Anabaptist confession

Anabaptists preach, teach and follow all the teachings of King Jesus, including:

  • Absolute truthfullness Exodus 20:16
  • Not swearing oaths Matthew 5:33-37
  • Separation from the world II Corinthians 6:17
  • Permanence of marriage Genesis 2:24 Romans 7:2 Matthew 19:6 I Corinthians 7:10-15 Matthew 18:9
  • Rejection of self-defence Matthew 5:39-48 Matthew 10:23
  • Two kingdoms John 18:36 Matthew 6:24
  • Headship order I Corinthians 11:3
  • Women’s headcovering I Corinthians 11:5-6
  • Submission to church authority Hebrews 13:17
  • Equality of all persons Galatians 3:28
  • Believer’s baptism Mark 16:16 Acts 8:12 Acts 19:5
  • Servitude and humbleness Philippians 2:3 James 4:6
